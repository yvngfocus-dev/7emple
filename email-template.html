From the archive

Grizzlies Aftermovie
100K views. Zero paid promotion.

Last August we filmed a weekend basketball tournament in Zoetermeer. Two days of heat, energy, and people genuinely caring about something. The aftermovie went up on a Monday.

By the end of the week, clubs from France, Germany, and England had reached out about registrations. Three sponsors moved faster than usual. They all pointed at the video.

No paid promotion. No boosting. Just a film that got the feeling right.

“Content built with intent sells the next edition before you have to ask.”

That's the thing about video done properly — it doesn't just document what happened. It makes the next thing feel inevitable.

Quick take

Why most brand videos get ignored.

They're made to check a box. Finished product on a white background. Voiceover listing features. A logo at the end. No one asked for it, and no one shares it.

The videos that spread are the ones that make the audience feel something — recognised, inspired, understood. Film the people, not the product.
