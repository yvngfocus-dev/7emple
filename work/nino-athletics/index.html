The Challenge

What they
needed.

Nino had everything a great personal trainer needs. Results, knowledge, a no-nonsense approach. What he didn't have was a brand that communicated any of it. His socials were broad, his audience was vague, and his pricing wasn't matching the value he delivered.

When you try to speak to everyone, you reach no one. The right clients weren't finding him. Those who did sometimes dropped off before results arrived. And through all of it, Nino was charging rates that didn't reflect what he was actually worth.

He needed a complete personal trainer brand strategy. Positioning, landing page, content. Built to attract motivated entrepreneurs and professionals in the Netherlands who were serious about results.

Our Approach

  • 01 Positioning first. We defined Nino's sweet spot: driven entrepreneurs and professionals who want results without excuses. From there, every piece of brand content had a clear target and a sharp point of view. His bio, his copy, his visuals. All aligned.
  • 02 A conversion-led brand overhaul. We rebuilt his Instagram bio with a clear call-to-action, rewrote his landing page with conversion copy and a video sales letter, and created onboarding videos combining personality with structure. One for welcome, one for instruction.
  • 03 A content strategy built for consistency. We created a social media content strategy so Nino could show up with the right tone and message. Every post with direction, authority, and a clear reason to reach out.
