The Challenge

What
they needed.

Strength 2 Strength came to us as a personal trainer with a solid reputation but zero digital presence. No brand identity, no content system, and no way to attract new clients outside of referrals.

They needed a full brand foundation and a funnel that turns attention into booked sessions.

Our Approach

  • 01 Built a complete brand strategy and visual identity from scratch. Positioning, messaging, tone of voice. Everything aligned to attract serious clients.
  • 02 Shot a full brand photoshoot and produced a VSL that communicates the value before the first conversation even happens.
  • 03 Designed and built a high converting landing page as the core of the funnel. Social media drives traffic. The page books the free lesson.
  • 04 Optimised the existing website for SEO and user experience. Every page built to convert, every word written to rank.
  • 05 Took over full social media content management. Creation, scheduling, community engagement, and monthly reporting.

The Outcome

What we
delivered.

Six months in, Strength 2 Strength went from relying on word of mouth to running a full digital system. The funnel works on autopilot. Social media builds the audience. The landing page converts them. Free lessons get booked without a single DM.
